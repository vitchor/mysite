Have you ever realized that the way pictures are taken hasn't changed since the invention of the camera? It's time someone changed this old-boring picture format, huh?

We have in our hands state-of-the art technology for taking pictures - the iPhone camera. But still, all we do is point and shoot. One focus, one point of interest.

What if you wanted to show people the same image, but highlighting different regions - making each one of them unique at a time?

We present to you a brand-new format for taking pictures: dyfocus. With dyfocus, you can select up to three points of interest at a time when taking a picture. You'll get as a result an image that smoothly alternates the focus between the chosen points (regions) of interest.
